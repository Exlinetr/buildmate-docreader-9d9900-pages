Privacy Policy for DocReader

DocReader

This Privacy Policy explains how DocReader collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

Information Collection and Use

DocReader is designed to prioritize user privacy. The app requires access to your device's camera and photo gallery solely to allow you to select images for PDF conversion. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, photos, or created PDF documents to any external servers.

Local Data Processing

All image-to-PDF conversions are performed entirely on your device. Your images and documents are processed locally and are not uploaded to our servers, ensuring that your sensitive information remains private and secure under your own control.

Third-Party Services

DocReader does not share any personal information or document data with third-party services. The app operates independently on your device and does not integrate with tracking tools, analytics providers, or advertising networks that collect personal identifiable information.

Data Security

We take the security of your files seriously. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, the security of your files is protected by your device's built-in operating system security features. We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated to ensure maximum security.

Your Rights and Control

You retain full ownership and control over your files, photos, and generated PDF documents. You can grant or revoke the app's permission to access your camera and photo gallery at any time through your mobile device's system settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@docreaderapp.example.com.
