DocReader Privacy Policy

DocReader

Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how DocReader handles your information when you use our mobile application. We build tools that keep your data securely in your hands.

On-Device Local Processing

DocReader is designed to perform all image-to-PDF conversion processes locally on your device. We do not upload your images, documents, or personal files to external servers. Your source photos and generated PDFs never leave your mobile device, ensuring maximum confidentiality and security.

Device Permissions and Access

To provide our services, DocReader requests access to your device's camera and photo gallery. This access is strictly used to select and capture the images you wish to convert into PDF format. We do not monitor, store, or share your camera stream or gallery contents for any other purpose.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. Using DocReader does not require account creation, registration, or the sharing of personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. You can use all core features of the app completely anonymously.

Third-Party Data Sharing

Because DocReader operates locally and offline, we do not share, sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to external third parties. We do not employ third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs that gather or report user behavior data.

Data Security and Retention

Since we do not store your data on external servers, the security of your files relies on the security of your mobile device. Any PDFs you generate with DocReader are stored in your device's local storage and can be deleted by you at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend checking this page periodically for any changes. Updates are effective immediately upon being posted within the app or on our app store listing.

Contact and Inquiries

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled in DocReader, please contact us at privacy@docreaderapp.com.
