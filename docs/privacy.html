DocReader Privacy Policy

DocReader

DocReader respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile application. By using DocReader, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection and Local Processing

DocReader does not collect, store, or transmit your personal documents, photos, or generated PDF files. All processing, image scanning, and document conversions occur entirely on your local mobile device.

We do not have access to your camera feed or gallery contents. The permissions you grant to the app are used purely by the local operating system to supply selected images to the application for PDF creation.

2. Usage Information and Diagnostics

To improve the stability and performance of DocReader, we may collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable diagnostic and usage data. This includes information such as app crash logs, device manufacturer, operating system version, and general feature usage statistics.

This diagnostic information does not contain any personal data or details about the content of your documents.

3. How We Use Collected Data

Since we do not collect your personal files, we cannot and do not use them. Any anonymous diagnostic and usage information we receive is used solely to identify bugs, improve app stability, and optimize user experience.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or documents with third parties. Anonymous diagnostic data may be processed by service providers (such as crash-reporting and analytics services) to help us analyze app performance, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.

5. User Rights and Permissions

You can grant or revoke camera and photo gallery permissions at any time through your device's settings menu. Revoking these permissions will prevent DocReader from accessing new images, but will not affect the safety of your existing local data.

Because we do not store your data on our servers, we do not hold any personal files that you can request to access, modify, or delete.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us at privacy@docreaderapp.com.
