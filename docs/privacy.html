Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

DocReader (referred to as "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our mobile application.

By using DocReader, you agree to the conditions described in this policy. Because DocReader operates fully locally on your device, your document content is completely private and secure.

1. Information Collection and Use

DocReader does not collect, access, view, or store the contents of your documents, photos, or generated PDF files. All file creation, compilation, and modification activities are performed strictly on your physical device.

We do not maintain any cloud databases or servers that store your document data, ensuring that your sensitive personal documents remain in your custody at all times.

On-Device Processing Guarantee

Your photos, camera feeds, and generated PDF files remain strictly on your physical device. We have no technical mechanism to view, access, or intercept your documents.

2. Device Permissions

To provide its core features, DocReader requires access to certain system permissions on your device. You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time through your device's settings menu.

  • Camera: Used to capture real-time images of physical documents to be converted into PDF format.
  • Photo Gallery: Used to select existing photos or images from your device's library for PDF compilation.

3. Diagnostic and Analytics Data

We may collect anonymous, non-personal diagnostic data to monitor and improve the stability and performance of DocReader. This data does not contain any personal identifiers or details about the content of your documents.

  • Device hardware details (device model, operating system version, and screen resolution).
  • Anonymized crash logs and error statistics to help us troubleshoot bugs.
  • Aggregate usage statistics (such as the number of PDFs created or buttons clicked) to help us refine features.

4. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data. Because we do not collect your files, we do not store, retain, or have any backup of your generated PDFs.

Any PDFs you create are stored in your device's local memory. If you delete DocReader, any files stored exclusively within the app's secure private directory will be permanently deleted unless you have exported them to your device's general storage or a personal cloud backup.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately from our servers (if any analytic data is affected).

6. Security of Your Data

The security of your documents is highly protected because they never leave your device. However, the security of files stored on your device depends on your device's security status. We highly recommend configuring passcode, face recognition, or fingerprint lock settings to prevent unauthorized physical access to your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the revised policy on this page and updating the 'Last Updated' date. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support email provided in the App Store listing.
