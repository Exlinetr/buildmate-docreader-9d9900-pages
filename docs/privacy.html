DocReader Privacy Policy

DocReader

This Privacy Policy explains how DocReader collects, uses, and safeguards your information. By using our application, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring that your privacy is maintained.

On-Device Processing

We believe in absolute data privacy. DocReader processes all image-to-PDF conversions locally on your mobile device. None of your photos, camera captures, or generated PDF documents are uploaded, processed, or stored on our servers.

Data Collection and Permissions

To provide our conversion services, DocReader requests permissions to access your camera and photo gallery. This access is strictly used to select and capture images for the PDF generation process. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal identifiers or usage information.

Third-Party Services

DocReader does not share, sell, or disclose your personal data to any third-party entities. Because all processes run locally on your device, no external service providers have access to your documents or images.

Children's Privacy

Since DocReader does not collect or transmit personal information, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly compile or store any information from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights and Compliance

We respect data protection regulations globally, including GDPR and CCPA. Since we do not store your data on external databases, your local device settings and application data state govern your rights. You have complete control over your inputs and outputs at all times.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the policy within the app or on our store pages. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@docreaderapp.com.
