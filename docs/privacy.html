Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

DocReader ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is dedicated to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how our mobile application handles user data.

Because DocReader operates entirely locally, we do not collect, store, or share your documents, photos, or personal data on any servers. Please read the sections below to understand how your permissions are used.

1. Information Collection and Storage

DocReader does not collect, harvest, or transmit any personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers during standard use.

All image files, camera captures, and generated PDF documents are processed strictly within the App's local container on your device. We have no mechanism to view or access your files.

2. Required Permissions

To provide its core document-to-PDF conversion functionality, DocReader requests access to specific device capabilities. You can manage these permissions at any time through your device settings.

Camera Access

The camera permission is used solely to capture real-time images of documents. Images captured are immediately processed locally and are not saved unless you choose to compile them.

Photo Library Access

The photo library permission allows you to select existing pictures for conversion. The App only accesses files that you manually choose to import.

3. On-Device PDF Processing

Every step of the conversion workflow—including image loading, scaling, compressing, and compiling into PDF format—takes place directly on your mobile device.

This approach guarantees that your sensitive documents are never sent across the internet, protecting you from remote data breaches and interception.

On-Device Processing Security

DocReader operates with zero server interaction. Your images and documents are processed locally and never uploaded to the cloud, ensuring total privacy and control over your files.

4. Third-Party Analytics and Crash Reporting

We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, user tracking SDKs, or invasive analytics tools in DocReader.

Standard anonymous telemetry, such as application crash logs and installation statistics, may be collected and provided to us automatically by Google Play or the Apple App Store. These reports contain no personal identifiers, photo details, or document contents.

5. Data Retention Policy

Since we do not upload any user files to our servers, we do not retain, store, or archive your data.

All temporary image caches and processed PDFs are saved within your device's local application sandbox. If you delete the DocReader application, all related local files and configurations will be permanently removed from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. Since DocReader does not collect any personal information from any users, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information (such as via a support email), please contact us so we can delete that information immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy within the App.

We recommend checking this page regularly to stay informed about our privacy practices. Your continued use of DocReader signifies your acceptance of any updates.

8. Contact for Privacy Inquiries

If you have any questions, feedback, or concerns regarding your privacy while using DocReader, please contact us through the app store listing support email or our designated support address.
