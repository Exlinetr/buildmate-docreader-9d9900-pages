DocReader Privacy Choices

DocReader

At DocReader, we empower you to control your personal data. This page outlines the choices available to you regarding your information and how you can exercise your rights under regulations like GDPR and CCPA.

Managing App Permissions

You can manage DocReader's access to your camera and photo library at any time through your mobile device's system settings. Revoking these permissions will prevent the app from reading new images but will not affect previously generated PDFs on your device.

Accessing and Deleting Your Files

Because DocReader runs entirely locally, all generated PDF files are saved directly to your device's storage. You can access, share, rename, or permanently delete these files at any time using your device's default file manager or gallery. We do not keep copies of your documents.

Deleting Your App Data

To wipe all application state and settings, you can uninstall DocReader from your device. This action will clear any temporary cache files created during PDF conversion. Please ensure you have backed up any important PDFs to your local storage before uninstalling.

Opt-Out and Contact Requests

DocReader does not track, sell, or share your data with advertisers. Therefore, there are no opt-out mechanisms required for cross-site tracking or data sales. If you have specific inquiries regarding your privacy rights, please submit a request to privacy@docreaderapp.com.
