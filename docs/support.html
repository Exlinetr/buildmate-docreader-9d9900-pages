Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert my images into a PDF? To create a PDF, open DocReader and tap the option to add images. You can choose to capture a new photo using your device's camera or select existing images from your gallery. Once you have added and arranged your pages, tap the generate button to save your PDF document.

Are my documents and photos secure? Yes. Security and privacy are core features of DocReader. All image processing and PDF conversion tasks are performed locally on your device. Your photos, text, and generated PDF files are never uploaded to any remote server or shared with third parties.

Can I combine multiple photos into a single PDF? Yes. DocReader fully supports multi-page PDF generation. You can select multiple images from your photo library or take several pictures in sequence, and they will be compiled into a single PDF document in the order you specify.
