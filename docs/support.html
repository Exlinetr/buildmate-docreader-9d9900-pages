Welcome to the DocReader Support Center. We are here to help you get the most out of your document scanning and PDF conversion experience. If you cannot find the answer to your question below, please feel free to reach out to our team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does DocReader process my images? All PDF conversions are performed locally on your device. Your images and documents are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and speed.

Can I combine multiple images? Yes, DocReader allows you to select multiple images from your library or take multiple photos using your camera to compile them into a single PDF document.

Troubleshooting

If you experience issues accessing your camera or photo gallery, please check your device settings to ensure DocReader has the necessary permissions to access these services.

Contact Support

Need further assistance? You can contact our support team at support@docreaderapp.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24–48 hours.
