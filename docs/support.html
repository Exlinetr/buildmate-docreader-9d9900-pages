Welcome to the DocReader Support center. Our goal is to make digital paperwork as simple and hassle-free as possible. Below you will find answers to frequently asked questions and troubleshooting guides to help you make the most of DocReader.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert images to PDF using DocReader? To convert images, open the app and choose to either take a new photo using your camera or select existing images from your gallery. Once you have chosen your images, select the option to compile them into a PDF.

Where are my converted PDF files stored? All documents are processed and saved locally on your device. DocReader does not upload your files to any external servers, ensuring your documents remain completely private and secure.

Can I select multiple images at once? Yes, DocReader supports multi-page PDF generation. You can select multiple images from your library or capture multiple photos sequentially to combine them into a single PDF document.

Troubleshooting & Permissions

Why is DocReader asking for camera and storage permissions? DocReader requires access to your camera to take new photos and your storage/gallery to select existing images for conversion. Without these permissions, the app cannot access the images needed to create PDFs.

I cannot find my generated PDFs. What should I do? If you cannot locate your generated PDF files, check your device's default files or downloads folder, or review DocReader's save settings. Ensure your device has enough free storage space.
