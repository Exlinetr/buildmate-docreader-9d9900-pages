Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a PDF from multiple images? To convert multiple images, select the gallery or camera option, select all the images you want to include, rearrange them if necessary, and tap the convert button to generate your single PDF file.

Are my documents secure? Yes. DocReader processes all image-to-pdf conversions locally on your mobile device. Your files and photos are never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third parties.
